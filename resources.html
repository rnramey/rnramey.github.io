The Food Pantry:

The Food Pantry is open to all undergraduate and graduate students with no questions asked!! This organization is open, welcoming, and inclusive in helping students who are experiencing any degree of food insecurity.

Where? Gleeson Library Atrium

Phone? (415) 422-4099

Email? usfpantry@usfca.edu

Website? myusf.usfca.edu/food-pantry

For additional information check out the instagram @usfpantryinstagram.com/usfpantry

Food Recovery Network

A student-led non-profit organization that aims to fight against food waste and hunger within our SF community. This social justice club, on campus, takes perishable food that would otherwise be wasted and distributes it to those in need around the Mission and SF communities, prioritizing the unhoused and food insecurity.

For additional information, reach out to Mauricio Diaz de Leon at mdiazdeleon@usfca.edu

Other updates and events are posted on their Instagram: @usfcafrn

USF Garden

The USF Garden is a vibrant, all-inclusive community that promotes the growth and cultivation of local food in a college campus setting. Current garden manager, Story Gartland, says their favorite part of the garden is the rich community it creates. With an open and experiential teaching mindset, the garden is open for any students to grow, tend, and harvest!

Find more information on the USF Community Garden at their website: https://www.usfca.edu/arts-sciences/programs/undergraduate/urban-agriculture-minor/community-garden

USF Farm Stand

Held on Welch Field, the USF Farm Stand is roughly every second Tuesday of every month. All the food is harvested, collected, cooked, and prepared by students in the Community Garden Outreach class and is completely free for anyone (students, faculty, and community members alike). Because the food is free and made by students, there is a limit to how many people the Farm Stand can accommodate which is why it is not highly advertised. But the Farm Stand is welcome for all, so next time you are on campus on a Tuesday, come to Welch Field to see if the Farm Stand is around!
